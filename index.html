Whiringa Ltd, a female-owned, multiethnic enterprise, is dedicated to driving technological innovation and excellence in Marlborough. With a steadfast commitment to diversity, inclusivity, and honoring Te Tiriti o Waitangi, we are here to reshape the tech landscape.

Our mission is to create a vibrant tech community that reflects our values of diversity and innovation, fostering environments where ideas flourish and technology advances.
